About Gaza

The Gaza Strip is a narrow piece of land located along the Mediterranean Sea, bordered by Egypt and Israel.

  • Area: ~365 km²
  • Population: ~2.3 million (2023)
  • Population Density: Over 6,300 people per km² — among the highest in the world
  • Length: ~41 km | Width: 6–12 km
  • Governance: Administered by Hamas; subject to blockade by Israel and Egypt

This app helps visualize how the small size of Gaza compares to other regions in the world.
